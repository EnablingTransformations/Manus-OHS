# Video Ads Strategy for Optimal Health Summit

## Overview

Video ads have 80% higher engagement rates than static images on Facebook. This guide provides a production roadmap for creating high-converting video content.

---

## Video 1: 15-Second Teaser (Hero Video)

### Concept
Quick cuts of speakers, venue, and energy to create FOMO and curiosity.

### Script
```
[0-2s] Event venue shot + music starts
"One day can change everything."

[2-5s] Quick cuts of speakers presenting
"Join 150 health leaders..."

[5-8s] Networking/audience interaction
"...for transformative conversations..."

[8-12s] Key topics: brain health, longevity, hormones
"Longevity. Brain Health. Peak Performance."

[12-15s] CTA with date
"June 20, San Diego. Limited to 150 seats.
Get your ticket now."
```

### Production Tips
- Use fast cuts (0.5-1 second per shot)
- Add text overlays for key messages
- Include upbeat, energetic background music
- End with clear CTA button

### Where to Use
- Campaign 1 (Awareness) - Cold traffic
- Lookalike audiences
- Retargeting all website visitors

## Video 2: 30-Second Speaker Spotlight

### Concept
Feature one speaker with their credentials and key insight.

### Template (repeat for each speaker)

**Dr. Neville Campbell**
```
[0-3s] Speaker on stage
"Dr. Neville Campbell, MD, MBA, CEO"

[3-10s] Speaker talking (use actual footage if available)
"The future of health isn't about treating disease,
it's about optimizing performance."

[10-25s] Key benefits of attending
"Learn cutting-edge strategies from world-class experts
who've transformed thousands of lives."

[25-30s] CTA
"Hear from Dr. Campbell and 6 other world-class speakers.
June 20. Get tickets now."
```

### Production Tips
- Use high-quality footage (1080p minimum)
- Add speaker credentials as text overlay
- Include quote or key insight
- Show speaker photos/credentials

### Where to Use
- Campaign 2 (Consideration) - Website retargeting
- Interest-based audiences
- LinkedIn ads

## Video 3: 15-Second Testimonial/Success Story

### Concept
Quick testimonial from past attendee or speaker about impact.

### Script Template
```
[0-2s] Person on camera
"I attended the last summit..."

[2-10s] Their story
"...and it completely changed my approach to health.
I lost 30 pounds, reversed my prediabetes, and have
more energy than I've had in 20 years."

[10-15s] CTA
"Don't miss this opportunity. June 20.
Limited to 150 seats. Get your ticket today."
```

### Production Tips
- Use authentic testimonials (not overly polished)
- Include before/after photos if available
- Add captions for accessibility
- Use emotional, uplifting music

### Where to Use
- Campaign 2 (Consideration) - High-intent audiences
- Retargeting cart abandoners
- Past purchasers (upsell to VIP)

---

## Video 4: 6-Second Bumper Ad

### Concept
Ultra-short ad for mobile feeds and stories.

### Script
```
[0-2s] Event highlight
"Optimal Health Summit"

[2-4s] Key benefit
"One day. 150 leaders. Life-changing insights."

[4-6s] CTA
"June 20. Get tickets → [link]"
```

### Production Tips
- Keep text minimal (mobile viewers)
- Use bold colors and movement
- Include captions (many watch without sound)
- Make it loop-able

### Where to Use
- Facebook Stories
- Instagram Reels
- Mobile feed (all campaigns)

---

## Video 5: 30-Second Urgency/Scarcity Video

### Concept
Create FOMO with countdown and limited availability messaging.

### Script
```
[0-3s] Countdown timer on screen
"Only 10 days left"

[3-10s] Seat availability
"150 seats total. 47 remaining."

[10-20s] Price/value proposition
"Advanced Access General: $97
(Early pricing ends April 24)"

[20-30s] CTA
"Don't wait. Secure your spot now.
justdoitrightnow.com/tickets"
```

### Production Tips
- Use animated countdown timer
- Update seat count weekly
- Include urgency language ("Only X left")
- Add scarcity messaging

### Where to Use
- Campaign 3 (Conversion) - Cart abandoners
- Retargeting high-intent audiences
- Final 2 weeks before event

## Production Checklist

### Before Filming
- [ ] Write scripts for all 5 videos
- [ ] Scout filming locations (venue, speakers' offices)
- [ ] Schedule speaker availability
- [ ] Gather testimonials from past attendees
- [ ] Create storyboards for each video
- [ ] Source background music (royalty-free)

### During Filming
- [ ] Film in 1080p minimum (preferably 4K)
- [ ] Use good lighting (natural light preferred)
- [ ] Record clear audio (use lavalier mic for speakers)
- [ ] Get multiple takes of each scene
- [ ] Film B-roll of venue, networking, presentations
- [ ] Capture speaker headshots

### Post-Production
- [ ] Edit videos to exact lengths (15s, 30s, 6s)
- [ ] Add text overlays and captions
- [ ] Add background music and sound effects
- [ ] Color grade for consistency
- [ ] Export in multiple formats (MP4, MOV)
- [ ] Create vertical versions for Stories/Reels

### Quality Standards
- Resolution: 1080p minimum (preferably 1440p)
- Frame rate: 24fps or 30fps
- Audio: Clear, no background noise
- Captions: Burned-in or SRT file provided
- File size: < 100MB for Facebook upload

## A/B Testing Framework for Videos

### Test 1: Hook Type
**Variation A:** Problem-focused hook
- "Most people accept fatigue and aging as inevitable..."

**Variation B:** Solution-focused hook
- "Imagine having the energy and vitality of your 20s..."

**Variation C:** Curiosity-focused hook
- "What if everything you knew about health was wrong?..."

**Duration:** 3-5 days per variation  
**Metric:** Click-through rate (CTR)  
**Winner:** Highest CTR advances to next test

---

### Test 2: Video Length
**Variation A:** 6-second bumper
**Variation B:** 15-second teaser
**Variation C:** 30-second full story

**Duration:** 5-7 days per variation  
**Metric:** View-through rate (VTR)  
**Winner:** Highest VTR advances

---

### Test 3: Call-to-Action
**Variation A:** "Get Tickets"
**Variation B:** "Learn More"
**Variation C:** "Reserve Your Spot"

**Duration:** 5-7 days per variation  
**Metric:** Conversion rate  
**Winner:** Highest conversion rate becomes primary CTA

---

### Test 4: Thumbnail/Cover Image
**Variation A:** Speaker photo
**Variation B:** Venue photo
**Variation C:** Audience/networking photo

**Duration:** 5-7 days per variation  
**Metric:** Video plays (people clicking play)  
**Winner:** Highest play rate

---

### Test 5: Background Music
**Variation A:** Upbeat, energetic
**Variation B:** Inspirational, motivational
**Variation C:** Calm, professional

**Duration:** 5-7 days per variation  
**Metric:** Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)  
**Winner:** Highest engagement

## Video Performance Metrics

### Key Metrics to Track
| Metric | Target | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| View-Through Rate (VTR) | > 50% | If < 50%, shorten video or improve hook |
| Click-Through Rate (CTR) | > 1.5% | If < 1.5%, improve CTA or messaging |
| Cost Per View | < $0.05 | If > $0.05, refine audience targeting |
| Cost Per Click | < $0.50 | If > $0.50, improve ad creative |
| Conversion Rate | > 2% | If < 2%, optimize landing page |

### Daily Reporting
- Views and VTR
- Clicks and CTR
- Cost per action
- Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- Video completion rate by segment (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%)

## Quick Start: DIY Video Production

### Minimum Equipment Needed
- Smartphone (iPhone 12+ or Android flagship)
- Tripod ($20-50)
- Lavalier microphone ($30-100)
- Lighting (natural light or ring light $50-200)
- Laptop for editing

### Step-by-Step Process
1. Write script (5-10 minutes)
2. Record footage (30-60 minutes)
3. Edit video (1-2 hours)
4. Add captions (30 minutes)
5. Export and upload (15 minutes)

**Total time: 3-4 hours per video**
